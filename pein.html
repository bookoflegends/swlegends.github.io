Pein/Cratos
From the rubbles of Ruby, Pein is arguably the greatest fighter from the 08-10' era.
Mentored by Snake KO and ExileD/Vulcan KO, Pein became a character and player of his own right.
It wasn't long until he conquored Sapphire and Emerald as well under the alias Cratos.
Pein was particularly known to be a contender for #1 consistently and known to end long win streaks of any player.
From Exile, to Show, to Zinedine, Pein proved himself time and time again.
Most notably, Pein's most respected alias, Aeon was responsible for creating the elite group Lighter Than Air based in The Stone Circle world.
Pein honed his skills under the tutelage of Crisipo, and surpassed the rumored Shuyin/Lionheart/Combo-Riffs as well.
Pein was also the only fighter to have ended Show's almost year long undefeated streak.
His LtA organization became a very sought-for group, arguably more iconic than Riffs from 2009 to even as far as 2017.
Prior to the PM era, Pein was capable of critical hits without blocking via implementing multi hits in 3 second intervals (credits to Crisipo).
Had PM's not come in within that following week, Pein would have been the most deadly player the game has ever seen.
When the PM's era occured, Pein was among a year long power trio struggle for #1 with Exile and Zinedine.
Even today, Pein's name is still thrown in conversations among new-gens despite his era having been ended a long time ago.
Gene himself is well aware of Pein's antics and reputation of the past.
Arguably, Pein may be considered among the top 5 most famous players of Sherwood and one of the greatest of all time.
