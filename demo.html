SunsetWolf/Stutes
From the caves of Emerald, emerged a man with a powerful ambition on one fateful full moon.
SunsetWolf made his mark in FfL and by post PM's era truly changed the landscape and history of Sherwood.
Becoming an extremely formiddable fighter...
Sunsetwolf was among the top four, battling between #1 with Exile, Zinedine, and Pein during the height of PM ( Power Moves Era) Sherwood.
It wasn't long until SunsetWolf became #1 and has consistently maintained his spot and defended it all the way until 2016-17'.
At the height of SunsetWolf's peak, there was no one that could contend his defense nor offense.
When the knowledge of Orb-Hitting came to light, it was SunsetWolf who possessed unique and full mastery over it against everyone else.
Arguably, as Stutes, SunsetWolf commanded the greatest clan in Sherwood history; BC.
Bellicose Cavalcade was at large the most powerful clan in Sherwood history.
Albeit, it wasn't a clan.
It was an empire.
With Stutes' command, BC colonized many clans and annexed them.
Such a presence even demanded the attention of Gene, who made it his mission to deliberately ban all BC members.
SunsetWolf may very well have one of the greatest consistent #1 records in Sherwood history, if not, arguably, the greatest record.
Ultimately, SunsetWolf is by far one of the top 5 most famous players of Sherwood, one of the greatest leaders, and one of the greatest fighters of all time.
