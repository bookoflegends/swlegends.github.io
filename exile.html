Bigsteel/Exile
There couldn't be a name more archaic than even the dungeons of Sherwood. A name that permeates the history from the forest of Classic to the era of Dungeon.
Exile is arguably the most famous player. Bigsteel was the leader of many clans, most notably Dream Haunters, The Horde, and Knights Of Old. Consistently Exile
battled at #1 throughout his years, and is truly one of the greatest fighters of all time. His name eclipses all legends.
Prior to the removal of the multi-hit/doubles/triples era, Exile on record had a leading score against every player.
Even when power-moves came into play, Exile quickly adapted and impacted Sherwood with an iron-fist.
If there is any player that deserves to be called the greatest, it is probably Exile.
